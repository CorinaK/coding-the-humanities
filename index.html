Corina's uncoding Amsterdam

Info for tourists - And more - And even more
Ugly little shops deserve love, too. And so do tourists. Despised by nearly all locals, I feel a certain connection to them. Feeling at home in Amsterdam is specifically reserved for 'Amsterdammers' (although being born here is not a prerequisite). The only indigenous I photograph is hiding behind an umbrella and shades. Me in the middle. I'm not a visitor, but I also don't belong here. I'd rather hide from the rain as well, but there I go, walking around, a fake tourist. Pretending to watch buildings, shops and statues, but secretly spying on the wanderers.
